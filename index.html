About HexCarb

Building deployable carbon intelligence, not just lab samples

HexCarb Advanced Materials Private Limited develops nanocarbon systems across SWCNT, MWCNT, graphene, and hybrid architectures. We combine material science, process engineering, and AI-assisted iteration to help teams move from formulation trials to qualification-ready outcomes.

What we build

  • Nanotube and graphene powders for R&D and industrial validation.
  • Engineered dispersions and inks for coating, printing, and slurry routes.
  • Fibers, films, TIM, and composite-ready systems for real applications.

How we engage

  • Quote-first commercial model with application context from day one.
  • HexLab-backed characterization and process refinement support.
  • AI-assisted decision loops that help reduce trial cycles and improve handoff to deployment teams.
