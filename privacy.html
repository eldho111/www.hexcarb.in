Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you submit through contact and enquiry forms, including name, email address, and project details. We may also collect basic usage analytics such as page visits and CTA interactions.

2. How we use information

We use collected information to respond to enquiries, provide product recommendations, prepare quotations, improve website performance, and maintain communication related to potential collaborations or purchases.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers used for website hosting, analytics, and enquiry processing, only to the extent required to provide our services.

4. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand website usage trends and improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data retention

We retain enquiry and communication records as needed for business operations, legal compliance, and support continuity. You may request deletion subject to applicable legal obligations.

6. Contact us

For privacy requests or questions, contact info@hexcarb.in.
