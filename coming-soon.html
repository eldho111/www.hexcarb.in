AI for advanced carbon materials — from research to production

HexCarb™ AI Engine connects research, scale-up, and commercial decisions for carbon nanomaterials. One system that carries material knowledge from the lab through to industrial deployment.

The Problem

Most materials AI stops at discovery

Existing tools help find new materials. They don't help manufacture, qualify, or sell them.

Lab-only tools

Most AI in materials science optimises experiments but stops before manufacturing or commercial decisions.

Disconnected data

Research results, pilot outcomes, supplier inputs, and customer requirements live in separate systems.

Discovery ≠ deployment

A material has no commercial value until it can be produced at scale, qualified, and delivered reliably.

What It Does

Three layers, one engine

Research

Formulation reasoning, experiment planning, and synthesis of material knowledge across the carbon portfolio.

Scale-up

Connects lab results with manufacturing constraints, process parameters, and qualification requirements.

Commercial

Aligns material readiness with customer timelines, pricing, and supply decisions.

Why Now

Materials are becoming strategic

Semiconductors, batteries, aerospace, and thermal systems all depend on better materials delivered faster.

R&D is accelerating

Research cycles are getting shorter. The teams that learn fastest across experiments and constraints will lead.

Supply chains need speed

Customers expect qualified materials on shorter timelines. Slow scale-up is a competitive disadvantage.

Carbon is central

SWCNT, MWCNT, and graphene are critical to next-generation electronics, energy, and thermal management.
