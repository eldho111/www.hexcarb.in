HexCarb™ AI Engine

Material intelligence, from target to recipe.

The engine carries what HexCarb learns across every experiment — so it can design a formulation to hit your target, predict how it will behave before anyone touches a beaker, and pick the next experiment that teaches it the most. Try the core ideas below.

Enterprise

One engine across every program

Run it at company scale and the engine orchestrates many material programs at once. Each measurement — from any team — compounds into shared knowledge, so every other program starts smarter and moves faster. Toggle the shared engine off to watch siloed teams crawl.

  • Orchestration: parallel programs from design through qualification, in one view.
  • Compounding knowledge: every result improves the model for all teams.
  • Governance & traceability: predictions backed by validated, audited data.
  • Deploy your way: cloud, on-prem, or API into your existing LIMS and ERP.
The Loop
